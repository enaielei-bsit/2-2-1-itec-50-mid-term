University Mission

Cavite State University shall provide excellent, equitable and relevant educational opportunities in the arts, sciences and technology throught quality instruction and responsive research and development activites.

It shall produce professional, skilled and morally upright individuals for global competitiveness.

University Vision

The premiere university in historic Cavite recognized for excellence in the development of globally competitive and morally upright individuals.
