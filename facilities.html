Cavite State University Bacoor Campus have different facilities to cater different needs in different courses. One of these facilities is the Kitchen dedicated for HRM students.

Another facility present in the campus is the Computer Laboratory.

Criminology students also has their own facility to practice gun-shooting.

For events that require large space, CvSU's broad quadrangle is large enough to hold thousands of students.
